About ApexStay

Where Luxury Meets Adventure - Creating Unforgettable Experiences Since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010, ApexStay began as a vision to redefine luxury hospitality. Our founders, passionate travelers themselves, recognized the need for a destination that offered more than just a place to sleep - they envisioned a complete experience.

What started as a single boutique hotel has grown into a premier destination known for its unique blend of luxury accommodation and thrilling amenities. Our commitment to excellence has earned us numerous awards and the loyalty of guests from around the world.

Today, ApexStay continues to innovate, constantly adding new features and amenities to ensure every stay is memorable. From our professional go-karting track to our serene side park, we're committed to providing diverse experiences for all our guests.
